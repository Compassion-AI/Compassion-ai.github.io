Mental Health Assistant

Psychotherapists have a demanding job that requires a great deal of attention to detail. These skills can vary across individual therapists influencing the standard of care received by their clients. The mental health app we are developing is designed to help therapists improve and make uniform the standard of care they provide. It does so by deploying NLP tools to infer client/therapist emotional states and provide feedback to therapists. It aims to quantify an inherently qualitative exercise and provide objective measures of progress.
