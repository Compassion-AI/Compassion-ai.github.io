Welcome to Compassion AI

Empowering Therapists, Transforming Lives

We are dedicated to crafting AI driven consumer applications spanning industries such as healthcare, education, nonprofit, etc. Our vision is to make the world a better place for everyone - biological and artificial - through the collaborative power of AI. We treat AI as a partner in our quest because we believe AI can be a force for good. Our committment is to harness AI's potential in uplifting people globally, particularly the underserved. Our diverse team brings together years of experience and expertise in business development, finance, IT, project management and data science. Currently, we are excited about a NLP based mental health assistant that we are developing for mobile phones.

Mental Health Assistant

Psychotherapists have a demanding job that requires a great deal of attention to detail. These skills can vary across individual therapists influencing the standard of care received by their clients. The mental health assistant we are developing is designed to help therapists improve and make uniform the standard of care they provide.
